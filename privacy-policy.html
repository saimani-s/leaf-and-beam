Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 7, 2026

At Leaf & Beam, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, process, and protect your information when you visit our website, submit requests for site visits, or communicate with us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out forms on our website. This includes:

  • Personal Identifiers: Full Name.
  • Contact Information: Phone number and email address.
  • Project Location: Physical address, city, or state of the prospective project.
  • Project Specification Details: Project type (Residential, Commercial, Interiors, etc.) and any optional custom details you describe in the message fields.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to fulfill your requests and deliver our architectural and interior design services. Specifically, we use your details to:

  • Schedule and coordinate your free site visit and architectural inspection.
  • Generate and supply detailed design estimations and proposals.
  • Communicate project updates and respond to your questions.
  • Improve our customer service, website usability, and overall client experience.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your data is strictly used internally by the Leaf & Beam design and execution teams. We may share information only when required to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your details. Submissions are processed through secure, encrypted transmission channels to prevent unauthorized interception, and stored in protected environments with strict access controls.

5. Cookies

Our website is designed as a clean, static landing page. We do not use tracking cookies or compile behavioral profile data. Standard server logs or basic visitor counters may run to measure performance metrics, which contain no personally identifiable details.

6. Third-Party Links

Occasionally, at our discretion, we may include links to third-party services or sources (such as Google Maps or external design libraries) on our website. These third-party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites.

7. Consent

By using our site, you consent to our website's Privacy Policy.
